Step One

At the Kilo Store, you pay by weight, with clothing marked by colored labels indicating different price categories per kilo, such as for basics or premium items.

Step Two

After shopping, you take your items to the scale, where the weight determines the price. The lighter your items, the less you pay.

The price per kilo

  • Yellow €60
  • Green €45
  • Red €30
  • Orange €75
  • Blue €90
